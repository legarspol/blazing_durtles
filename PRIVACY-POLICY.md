Effective date: 2026-06-24

Introduction
Lukewarm Durtles (“us”, “we”, or “our”) operates as a third-party client application for the WaniKani service. This developer and publisher of the application is the Lukewarm Durtles open source contributor ("developer"). This privacy policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Lukewarm Durtles Android application (the “App”).

We take your privacy seriously and are committed to ensuring that your personal information is protected. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect
The only information the App collects is your WaniKani API key. This API key is necessary for accessing WaniKani’s service and enabling the functionality of the App.

The App does not collect, store, or share any other personal information, and it does not collect any analytics, usage data, or other identifiers.

How Your Information is Used
Your WaniKani API key is used solely to authenticate your access to WaniKani’s services and to fetch data necessary for the App’s functionality. The API key is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to or shared with any third parties.

Data Sharing
The App does not share your API key or any other data with any third parties. As a client for the WaniKani service, the App only interacts with WaniKani’s API to provide its features.

We are not responsible for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party sites or services.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
